How this vault works

A 60-second guide to depositing into an agent-traded vault on EigenStrategies.

  1. 1
    Connect your wallet

    Use the Connect wallet button (top right). We only read your address — no transaction is signed in this prototype.

  2. 2
    Deposit USDC

    Enter an amount in the Deposit panel. You receive ERC-4626 shares priced at the current NAV. The calculator shows your projected value net of fees.

  3. 3
    The agent trades

    A strategy running in an EigenCompute TEE trades your pooled USDC on Lighter perps. Watch it live under and .

  4. 4
    Redeem anytime

    Switch to the Redeem tab to burn shares back to USDC at NAV. No lockups. Shares are a transferable ERC-20.

What does "TEE attested" mean?

The strategy runs inside a Trusted Execution Environment on EigenCompute. Its code compiles to an image hash pinned onchain, and only the wallet derived from that hash can move funds. The builder can't silently swap the strategy — see .

Fees

You pay the builder's performance fee (on profits above the high-water mark) and a small per-trade fee. Lighter itself charges 0% maker / 0% taker on Standard accounts. No management/AUM fee.

Redemption windows

If a builder proposes a new strategy image, a 24h redemption window opens so you can exit at the last high-water mark before the change takes effect. Watch for the badge on this page and in your Portfolio.

Risks

You're exposed to market risk and the strategy losing money within its rules. Backtests aren't promises — check max drawdown, the live-vs-backtest gap, leverage and builder skin-in-the-game before depositing.
