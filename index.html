How it works

Three moving parts. One trust anchor.

01 / CUSTODY
ERC-4626 vault

USDC sits onchain. Investors mint shares; redemptions clear from free balance or pull from Lighter.

02 / EXECUTION
EigenCompute TEE

Strategy runs in an attested container. KMS-derived wallet is the only key with trade authority.

03 / SETTLEMENT
Lighter sub-account

Each vault owns one Lighter sub-account. PnL flows back through the bridge to the vault.
